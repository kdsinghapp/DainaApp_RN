# Daina App Version 2

## Overview

Daina App Version 2 is focused on improving the complete delivery experience for customers, drivers, and admin.

The goal of this update is to make the app:

* Faster
* More secure
* Easy to use
* More reliable
* Better for daily delivery operations

This upgrade will improve customer experience, driver performance, and admin control.

---

# Customer Features

## 1. Live Parcel Tracking

Customers can track their parcel in real-time from pickup to delivery.

This improves transparency and builds trust.

---

## 2. Schedule Delivery

Users can schedule deliveries for future dates and times.

Options:

* Same Day
* Next Day
* Custom Date & Time

This helps in planned deliveries.

---

## 3. Fare Estimate Before Booking

Customers can view estimated charges before confirming the booking.

This gives price clarity.

---

## 4. Vehicle Selection

Customers can select the vehicle according to parcel size.

Available options:

* Bike
* Scooter
* Car
* Mini Van

This makes delivery flexible.

---

## 5. Saved Addresses

Users can save frequent addresses.

Examples:

* Home
* Office
* Shop

This makes repeat bookings faster.

---

## 6. Repeat Order

Customers can quickly repeat previous orders.

This saves time.

---

## 7. Parcel Type Selection

Customers can select parcel category.

Types:

* Documents
* Food
* Medicine
* Electronics
* Clothes
* Fragile Items

This helps in proper parcel handling.

---

## 8. Receiver Tracking Link

Customers can share parcel tracking with the receiver.

This improves visibility.

---

## 9. Delivery History

Users can view all previous completed and cancelled orders.

This makes order tracking easier.

---

# Driver Features

## 1. Smart Order Assignment

Orders will automatically assign to the nearest available driver.

This reduces waiting time.

---

## 2. Driver Dashboard

Drivers can track:

* Total Orders
* Completed Orders
* Earnings
* Bonus
* Working Hours

This improves performance tracking.

---

## 3. Route Optimization

The app will show the best and fastest route.

Benefits:

* Saves fuel
* Saves time
* Faster delivery

---

## 4. Multi Delivery Option

Drivers can deliver multiple orders in one trip.

This increases earnings.

---

## 5. Document Reminder

Drivers will receive reminders before important documents expire.

Examples:

* Driving License
* Insurance
* RC

This keeps driver records updated.

---

## 6. Order Accept / Reject

Drivers can accept or reject delivery requests.

This gives flexibility.

---

## 7. Driver Subscription Plans

Drivers can choose subscription plans.

Options:

* Weekly Plan
* Monthly Plan

This gives access to more delivery opportunities.

---

## 8. Driver Performance Graph

The app will show driver performance in graph format.

Available:

* Daily Graph
* Weekly Graph
* Monthly Graph

Example:

If the driver completes 5 rides in one day, the graph will show total rides and total earnings.

This helps drivers track progress.

---

## 9. Target Reward System

Drivers can earn rewards after completing targets.

Examples:

* 5 Rides = Bonus
* 20 Rides = Extra Reward
* 50 Rides = Special Reward

This keeps drivers motivated.

---

# Payment Features

## 1. Online Payment

Available payment options:

* UPI
* Card
* Wallet
* Cash

Easy and secure payment process.

---

## 2. Wallet System

Wallet can be used for:

Customer:

* Payments
* Refunds

Driver:

* Earnings
* Bonus

This makes payment handling easier.

---

## 3. Auto Invoice

Invoice will be generated automatically after every completed order.

Includes:

* Order Details
* Pickup Address
* Drop Address
* Fare
* Payment Method

This helps in maintaining records.

---

# Security Features

## 1. Proof of Delivery

Driver can upload:

* Delivery Photo
* Receiver Signature
* Receiver Name

This helps in delivery confirmation.

---

## 2. Parcel Image Upload

Customers can upload parcel image before pickup.

This helps the driver identify the parcel.

---

## 3. Secure Order Verification

Every order will have proper verification and tracking.

This improves trust and service quality.

---

# Support Features

## 1. In-App Chat

Customers and drivers can directly chat inside the order.

This improves communication.

---

## 2. Quick Support

Users can raise support tickets for:

* Delivery Issues
* Payment Problems
* Refund Requests
* Driver Complaints

This improves support quality.

---

# Admin Panel Features

Admin can create:

* Promotions

This improves customer engagement.

---

## 2. Zone-Based Pricing

Admin can set different pricing for different areas.

This gives better pricing control.

## 3. Order Monitoring

This improves control over operations.
